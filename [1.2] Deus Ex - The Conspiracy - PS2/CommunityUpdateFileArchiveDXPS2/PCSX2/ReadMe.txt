Self Extracting EXE for PCSX2 r4600.

Included is the nuvee keyboard and mouse emulator plugin, which emulates a keyboard and mouse plugged into a PS2.

This allows you direct keyboard / mouse control.

Launch PCSX2 via pcsx2-r4600-usb.exe and select Nuvee as the USB plugin. 

You may need to configure, particularly aquiring your mouse.
